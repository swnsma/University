Basic Installation
==================

1. Unzip the files and place them on your webserver.

2. Apply "addressbook.sql" to your Database.

3. Change "config/config.php" according to your database.



Advanced Tuning (optional)
==========================

4. (optional) Feel free to change all other parameters of "config/config.php".

5. (optional) Use the "mod_auth" and "mod_rewrite" samples for 
              your user management.

6. (optional) Contribute to the project (correct/add translations, etc.).
